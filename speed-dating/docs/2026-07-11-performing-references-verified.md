## The source study

Confirmed via WebSearch (Oxford Academic / RePEc / Columbia Business School): the paper is
Fisman, Iyengar, Kamenica & Simonson (2006), "Gender Differences in Mate Selection: Evidence
From a Speed Dating Experiment," The Quarterly Journal of Economics, vol. 121, issue 2, pages
673-697, DOI 10.1162/qjec.2006.121.2.673. Supports: the study description (Columbia speed
dating experiment, ~2002-2004, 21 waves, graduate students), and corroborates the direction
of the findings (women more selective; men respond more to physical attractiveness).

APA 7:
Fisman, R., Iyengar, S. S., Kamenica, E., & Simonson, I. (2006). Gender differences in mate
selection: Evidence from a speed dating experiment. The Quarterly Journal of Economics,
121(2), 673-697. https://doi.org/10.1162/qjec.2006.121.2.673
