## Indonesia's 2026 qualifying run and naturalization

Source fetched: Al Jazeera, "Indonesia and Kluivert part ways after FIFA World Cup 2026
miss," by Kevin Hand, 2025-10-16.
Confirmed statements:
- Indonesia reached "the fourth round of Asian Football Confederation (AFC) qualifiers"
  before elimination.
- Historic: the campaign took the team "to the final stages of qualification for the first
  time since 1938, when it appeared at the World Cup as Dutch East Indies."
- Naturalization: the federation was "naturalising players from Europe with Indonesian
  heritage"; "The majority of Kluivert's final roster of 23 were born overseas, mainly in
  the Netherlands."
- Patrick Kluivert appointed January 2025 on a two-year contract, departed October 2025
  after eight matches (three wins, four losses, one draw).
- Elimination came via "defeats by Iraq and Saudi Arabia."

Essay usage: safe to say Indonesia reached the final round of Asian qualifying for the
first time since 1938, on a squad mostly born in the Netherlands and naturalized for
Indonesian heritage, coached by Kluivert. Do NOT name specific Eredivisie players from this
source (it does not name them); if naming players is wanted, add a second source.
